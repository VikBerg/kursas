Avocado toast

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripped avocado
  • 2 peaces of toast bread
  • dash of the lemon juice
  • salt, paper, other spicies of your taste

Portions: 2

Preparation:
Toast 2 peaces of bread. Cut avocado in halve, remove seed and put it in a bowl. Mash avocado with a folk, add lemon juice, salt, paper and spycies. Destribute on the toasted bread. That's it, enjoy!
